How To Play

Each selected game costs the entry amount. A player can pick one game or many games from the active match day, but only one pick is allowed per game. Bets lock 15 minutes before the first game of that match day starts.

Every completed match day adds entry money into one total pot. At the end, total points decide the standings: 1st place gets 50% of the pot, 2nd place gets 30%, and 3rd place gets 20%. Everyone else gets no payout. Final net is payout minus entry money paid.

Favorite ChallengeFavorite wins by 2+ gives 6 points, by 3+ gives 8 points.

Result: 1 = Team 1 wins, X = tie, 2 = Team 2 wins.

Double: 1X, X2, or 12. Goals: O2 is more than 2 total goals, U2 is 2 or fewer total goals. GG: both teams score. NG: one or both teams do not score.

1st half: halftime result uses 1, X, 2. Halftime goals use O1.5 for 2+ first-half goals, U1.5 for 0 or 1 first-half goal.

Exact score: enter the full-time score, for example 3 - 1. The grey 0 - 0 is only a placeholder.

Favorite Challenge: some games may show extra options when one team is a strong favorite. If you choose “favorite by 2+”, that team must win by at least 2 goals. If you choose “favorite by 3+”, that team must win by at least 3 goals.

Solo correct bonus: if only one player gets a specific game correct, that player gets the regular points for that pick plus 2 extra points.

Perfect day bonus: if a match day has more than one game and every bet a player selected is correct, that player gets 4 extra points.

Ties: tied players split the prize for the tied places. If two players tie for 1st, they split the 1st and 2nd place money together.

June 10 friendly games stay in history for testing, but they do not count for points or money.
